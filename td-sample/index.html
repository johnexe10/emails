Not All ETFs Are Created Equal
At TD Asset Management, ESG is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that, as investors in a broad array of investment classes, we have a significant role to play in being a positive influence for continued improvement in ESG. That's why we maintain a future-focused investment strategy with an integrated investment approach to environmental, social and governance factors. Moreover, thoroughly considering ESG factors in investment strategies provides a more robust view of potential risks and opportunities-creating investment opportunities that are better for portfolios, and better for the planet.

These ETFs invest in stocks and bonds that have strong ESG metrics and leverage exclusive Morningstar Indexes and research from Sustainalytics, a Morningstar® company and a globally recognized leader in ESG risk ratings and research.
TD Equity ESG ETFs
Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) issues can pose material financial risks for companies. TD Equity ESG ETFs track the performance of market indexes that measure the investment return of large and mid-cap issuers with lower ESG risk ratings relative to their peers. All at one of the lowest fee rates in Canada.
